Hello World

Hey whats up my name is Tony and im here to share some insights that I've learned over the years on how to travel on a budget and have an amazing experience in seeing new places and meeting new people. My methods might be a little unconventional for some of us but nevertheless the way i go about it has made me more humble and grateful to have the life that i have been given.

Getting Started on your new adventure

The first step your going to be doing is choosing where you are going to travel. My advice would be to choose a place you have been always dreaming of going, go big or go home right?. After you made a final decision look at flights compare prices on different dates and see what would work best for you. I personally use skyscanner.com its simple and reliable.After your flight has been purchased i usually look up places i will spend the night usually stay in hostals they are very affordable and a great place to meet people
